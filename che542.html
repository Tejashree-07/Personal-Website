CUSTOM MICROREACTOR DESIGN THROUGH 3D PRINTING

As part of a course project in Chemical Reaction Engineering, I led a team exploring the use of 3D printing in reactor design to improve performance and functionality. We investigated how additive manufacturing enables the creation of complex geometries, enhances heat transfer, and reduces material waste. Our work included analyzing advanced techniques like 3D+G Printing, which applies metallic coatings to 3D-printed components for improved durability and thermal stability.
