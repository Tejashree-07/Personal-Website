Designing for Manufacturing: FEA-Based Optimization of a PLA Chassis

As part of a multidisciplinary academic project team of chemical, materials, and mechanical engineering students, I worked on a course project where I was involved in the simulation and manufacturing development of a lightweight PLA chassis for an RC car. conducting finite element analysis (FEA) in ANSYS to evaluate the structural integrity of multiple design variants under static loading. Based on these simulations, I collaborated on selecting the optimal chassis geometry and optimized print parameters for extrusion-based additive manufacturing (FDM). I contributed through the design, simulation, and 3D printing stages, with electronics integration handled separately by the mechanical engineering students.
