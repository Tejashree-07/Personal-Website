Hi, I'm Tejashree!
A problem-solver with a passion for technology and innovation! Let's team up to solve complex engineering challenges together!!

I am a recent MS Chemical Engineering graduate from the University of Southern California with a passion for materials innovation, process development and process optimization. With a strong foundation in Chemical Engineering, I enjoy tackling challenges at the intersection of materials science, data analytics, and advanced simulation.

While working on a research on thermal interface materials for electronics at USC, I found myself asking deeper questions: How can we manage heat more effectively in compact devices? How do material properties influence system reliability? These questions led me toward the semiconductor industry—where performance and precision go hand in hand.

That interest grew as I worked on real research problems—balancing experimental insights with modeling, and navigating trade-offs between performance, manufacturability, and scalability. I began to learn how even small improvements in materials or processes could make a measurable difference at the system level. This perspective drew me toward roles in process engineering and integration, where both technical depth and big-picture thinking are essential.

As I step into the professional world, I’m eager to apply my skills to solve impactful problems in semiconductor technology and beyond. The future is built by problem-solvers—and I’m ready to be one of them!
