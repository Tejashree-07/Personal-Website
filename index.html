Hi, I'm Tejashree!
A problem-solver with a passion for technology and innovation! Let's team up to solve complex engineering challenges together!!

I’m a recent M.S. Chemical Engineering graduate from the University of Southern California, with a strong interest in process engineering, manufacturing systems, and data-driven optimization. I enjoy solving complex engineering problems that require a combination of fundamental science, systems-level thinking, and practical execution.

Through academic projects and industry experience, I’ve worked at the intersection of materials behavior, process design, and simulation, using tools such as Aspen HYSYS, statistical analysis, and design-of-experiments (DOE) to improve process efficiency, performance, and scalability. My work emphasizes understanding how process parameters, equipment constraints, and material behavior interact to impact yield, efficiency, reliability, and scalability.

As I begin my professional career, I’m eager to contribute to teams focused on process development, yield improvement, and manufacturing optimization, particularly in semiconductor fabrication, automotive manufacturing, battery systems, and advanced materials processing!
